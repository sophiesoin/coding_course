Madagascar

Director:Erin Darnell and Tom McGrath

Synopsis:A group of animals who have spent all their life in a New York zoo end up in the jungles of Madagascar, and must adjust to living in the wild.

Personal Review: I personally believe "Madagascar" is a fun and hilarious movie for everyone to enjoy. It definately leaves you in tears of laughter. I love how it shows how friendship begins and how important friendship is.

Madagascar 3

Director:Conrad Vernon, Erin Darnell and Tom McGrath

Synopsis: The Madagascar animals join a struggling European circus to get back to New York, but find themselves being pursued by a psychotic animal control officer.

Personal Review:In its unassuming and unpretentious way, this is tremendous entertainment, bursting with colour and light and energy, and a great script from Eric Darnell and Noah Baumbach.
