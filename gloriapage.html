Gloria The hippo!

<

Gloria is one of the main characters in the films Madagascar, Madagascar: Escape 2 Africa and Madagascar 3: Europe's Most Wanted. She is the sole female of the quartet and acts as a motherly figure to the males (at least until she becomes Melman's love interest).
